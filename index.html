CBC Curriculum

Learning That Goes
Beyond the Classroom

The Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) is Kenya's modern approach to education — designed to develop the whole child, not just prepare them for exams. At Zion Hill, we embrace CBC fully across all our grade levels.

Instead of rote memorisation, children learn through doing, exploring, and creating — building real skills they'll carry through life.

Enroll Your Child

The 6 Core CBC Competencies

Critical Thinking & Problem Solving Children learn to analyse, question and find solutions independently
Communication & Collaboration Speaking, listening, teamwork and expressing ideas clearly
Imagination & Creativity Arts, music, storytelling — creativity is treated as a core skill
Citizenship Values, integrity, respect and pride in being Kenyan
Digital Literacy Preparing children for a technology-driven world confidently
Learning to Learn Self-awareness, curiosity and the love of lifelong learning
Curriculum

Explore CBC Subjects

Literacy & Language

Children develop strong reading, writing and communication skills in both English and Kiswahili. Through storytelling, phonics and creative writing they build a lifelong love for language.

  • Reading & Phonics
  • Creative Writing
  • Oral Communication
  • Kiswahili Language

Mathematics & Numeracy

Practical, real-world mathematics that makes numbers fun. Children learn through games, manipulatives and problem-solving that build strong logical thinking.

  • Number Sense & Operations
  • Geometry & Measurement
  • Data Handling
  • Problem Solving

Science & Technology

Curiosity-driven exploration of the natural world. Children conduct simple experiments, observe nature and learn how technology shapes everyday life.

  • Living Things & Environment
  • Matter & Energy
  • Simple Experiments
  • Basic Digital Literacy

Creative Arts & Crafts

Every child is an artist. Through drawing, painting, music, drama and craft, children express themselves and build confidence and emotional intelligence.

  • Visual Arts & Drawing
  • Music & Rhythm
  • Drama & Role Play
  • Craft & Design

Social Studies & CRE

Building great citizens. Children learn about Kenya, their community, culture and values — rooted in Kenyan identity and Christian faith.

  • Kenyan History & Culture
  • Community & Environment
  • Christian Religious Education
  • Citizenship & Values

Physical Education & Health

Active, healthy, happy children. Regular PE, sports and health education build physical fitness, teamwork and healthy habits for life.

  • Sports & Athletics
  • Games & Teamwork
  • Personal Hygiene
  • Health & Nutrition
